## Future Website Updates

The whole point of a personal website is that it reflects who you are.

Since I'm always changing, the site needs to evolve with me.

- **Skills:** As I unlock new abilities, I'll update the Skill Tree.
- **Projects:** Every new build goes into the Project Grid.
- **Lore:** If my career or status changes, the "Character Lore" gets patched.
- **Writing:** I'll be adding dev logs and essays consistently.

Luckily, changing the website is actually not too hard. I'd say the most annoying part is optimizing the images; everything else like adding a new project, adding a new skill, adding a project, or even creating a blog post is all pretty straightforward.
